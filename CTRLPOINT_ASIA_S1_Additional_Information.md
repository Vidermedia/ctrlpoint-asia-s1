## 1. Direct VRS Invites

### 1.1 VRS List

CTRLPOINT ASIA Season 1 uses the **Asia VRS** list as the basis for all Direct VRS Invites. The Asia VRS is not subdivided for invitation purposes in this tournament.

### 1.2 VRS Publication Dates

- **Invite VRS Publication:** The June 2026 Asia VRS publication, used as of the Invite Date.
- **Seeding VRS Publication:** The June 2026 Asia VRS publication.

### 1.3 Stage 2 (Playoffs) Direct VRS Invites

- **Number of Rosters Invited:** 8
- **Selection Method:** Invitations begin with the #1 ranked Roster on the Asia VRS as of the Invite Date and proceed sequentially down the list, skipping any Roster ranked higher than 12 on the Global VRS, until 8 Rosters have accepted.

### 1.4 Stage 1 (Swiss Group Stage) Direct VRS Invites

- **Number of Rosters Invited:** 10
- **Selection Method:** Invitations begin sequentially from the rank immediately following the lowest-ranked Stage 2 Direct VRS Invite, proceeding down the Asia VRS until 10 Rosters have accepted.

### 1.5 Invite Date

**June 10, 2026.**

### 1.6 Invitation Acceptance Window

Invited Rosters must respond within **3 working days** of the Invite Date. Failure to respond within this window constitutes declination.

### 1.7 Decline Cascade

If any invited Roster cannot accept or chooses to decline, Tournament Operator shall proceed sequentially down the Asia VRS until all announced invitation slots are filled.

For Stage 2 declines: cascade continues from the lowest filled Stage 2 rank, proceeding to the next available rank on the Asia VRS.

For Stage 1 declines: cascade begins from the rank immediately following the lowest filled Stage 1 rank.

If insufficient eligible Rosters exist on the Asia VRS to fill all invitation slots, remaining slots are reallocated to Open Qualifiers.

## 2. Open Qualifiers

### 2.1 Structure

Each sub-region runs two (2) Open Qualifier brackets — Qualifier #1 and Qualifier #2 — each producing 1 advancement slot to Stage 1, for a total of 2 advancement slots per sub-region and 6 advancement slots across all sub-regions.

| Sub-Region | Qualifier #1 Slots | Qualifier #2 Slots | Total Advancement Slots |
|------------|-------------------|-------------------|-------------------------|
| Oceania | 1 | 1 | 2 |
| West Asia | 1 | 1 | 2 |
| East Asia | 1 | 1 | 2 |

Open Qualifier eligibility is determined by region. Rosters must have a plurality of citizenships from the relevant sub-region (at least 3 of 5 players must hold citizenship from a country in that sub-region).

**East Asia:**

## 6. Integrity Rules and Invite Eligibility

The following objective criteria apply equally to all potential participants and serve as basis for ineligibility from Direct VRS Invites and Open Qualifier participation:

### 6.1 VAC and Anti-Cheat Bans

No Roster including a player with an active Valve Anti-Cheat (VAC) ban in any Counter-Strike title is eligible for invitation or qualification.

### 6.2 Active Competitive Bans

No Roster including a player with an active competitive ban from any Tier 1 or Tier 2 sanctioned tournament within the preceding 12 months is eligible for invitation or qualification.

### 6.3 Match-Fixing Sanctions

No Roster under active match-fixing investigation or sanction by any recognized esports integrity body is eligible for invitation or qualification.

### 6.4 One Roster Per Organization

Only one Roster per organization may participate. If an organization holds multiple Rosters, only the first to accept an invitation or qualify is eligible.

### 6.5 Application

These criteria are objective, published in advance, and apply equally to all potential participants. They are evaluated as of the Invite Date for Direct VRS Invites and as of registration for Open Qualifier participation.

For reference. Detailed format rules, seeding methodology, tiebreakers, and match procedures are published in the separate Rulebook at https://github.com/Vidermedia/ctrlpoint-asia-s1/blob/main/rulebook.md.

- **Participants:** 16 teams (8 Direct VRS Invites + 8 Stage 1 advancers)

- **Schedule:** July 16–20, 2026

### 7.3 Server and Anti-Cheat

- **Server:** Singapore (SGP) for all matches
- **Anti-Cheat:** FACEIT Anti-Cheat (mandatory)
